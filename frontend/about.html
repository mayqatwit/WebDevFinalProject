Our Mission

KitchenSync aims to streamline your cooking experience by providing easy access to any of your family's recipes. We allow for cross platform sharing of recipes, so you can access your favorite meals from any device, at any time. Our goal is to make cooking a collaborative and enjoyable experience for everyone, whether you're a seasoned chef or just starting out in the kitchen.
